        <h2>The Appeal of Stitch</h2>
        <p>Experiment 626, better known as Stitch, remains one of the most beloved characters in animation history. His design—created by Chris Sanders—is deceptively simple, utilizing rounded shapes and a distinct blue color palette that makes him instantly recognizable. This simplicity makes him an excellent subject for digital wallpapers, as he translates well across various screen sizes and resolutions.</p>
